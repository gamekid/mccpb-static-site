2025 Speakers

Deacon Harold Burke-Sivers

2025 - Keynote Speaker

Deacon Harold Burke-Sivers – known around the world as the “Dynamic Deacon” – is one of the most sought-after speakers in the Church today. He is a powerful and passionate evangelist and preacher, whose no-nonsense approach to living and proclaiming the Catholic faith will challenge and inspire those who hear him.

He travels across the United States and around the world speaking at conferences, workshops, retreats, parish missions, high schools, and young adult events – in short, to everyone who desires to know Jesus intimately and enjoy a deeper personal relationship with Him. His areas of expertise include marriage and family life, discerning God’s will, the sacraments, male spirituality, evangelization, prayer, and many others.

Deacon Harold holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Economics and Business Administration from the University of Notre Dame, and a Master of Theological Studies Degree from the University of Dallas. He is the host of Beacon of Truth on EWTN Radio, has appeared in the major motion picture Power in My Hands, and his video reflections on the Mass readings are featured by the US Conference of Catholic Bishops.

In addition, he is the host or co-host of several popular television series on EWTN and is featured on the renowned Chosen faith formation program by Ascension Press. Deacon Harold is an award winning author who has written six books, including Behold the Man: A Catholic Vision of Male Spirituality, Our Life of Service: The Handbook for Catholic Deacons, and Building a Civilization of Love: A Catholic Response to Racism. Retaining a deep love of Benedictine spirituality which he gained during his time discerning a call to that religious community, Deacon Harold is a Benedictine Oblate of Mount Angel Abbey and a member of the Fellowship of Catholic Scholars.

Learn more about Deacon Harold Burke-Sivers – deaconharold.com

Doug Hinderer

2025 - Special Guest

Doug is a licensed marriage and family therapist. In July of 2017, he received an MA in Marriage and Family Therapy. He maintains a limited private practice in which he provides tele-therapy throughout Illinois. In addition to seeing couples and individuals experiencing marital disharmony, he frequently speaks on a variety of marriage topics.

Doug hosts his own show “Marriage Unhindered” on Relevant Radio, Monday through Friday at 5:00pm central time. You can learn more about Doug and his on-line workshops “Marriage Tune-up” for married couples and “Marriage Discernment” for couples contemplating marriage by visiting his website – happymarriageforlife.com and you can follow him on Instagram and Facebook @happymarriageforlife.

Doug has also just published his first book – Rekindled Hearts: A Spiritual Journey to Marital Healing through Scepter Publishing.

Doug has been married to his wife Shirley for 45 years. They have 9 children and 10 grandchildren. Prior to his career in marriage counseling, Doug worked for 36 years in Human Resources. After 25 years with the National Association of REALTORS®, he retired in 2016 as the Senior Vice President, Human Resources and Association Leadership Development.
