Mission Statement

In the spirit of Pope Francisâ€™s renewed call for the New Evangelization, the mission of the McHenry County Catholic Prayer Breakfast is to provide an annual venue for Catholics to gather in fellowship and community prayer, and to participate in the evangelization which restores and strengthens Christ in the hearts of the faithful. We seek to promote a deeper understanding of our faith through exposure and access to exceptional Catholic evangelizers, resulting in greater bonds of unity as we witness the Joy of living the Gospel in our lives.
